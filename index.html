Coffeefx

CSS3 no JavaScript just GPU

Seconds

      date = new Date()
      segs = date.getSeconds()
      deg = segs * 6

      coffeefx('seg')
            .timing('linear')
            .iteration('infinite')
            .duration('60s')
              .from()
                .rotate(deg)
                .pop()
              .to()
                .rotate(360 + deg)
              .pop()
          .end()
